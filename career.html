Press Metal Family

Our
People

Press Metal is an Equal Opportunity Employer and we welcome talent from all walks of life to consider full time employment with us. We surround ourselves with People who are passionate in what they do and Our People are our strength and driving force to take our organisation forward.

Our
Innovation

Press Metal is the Largest Integrated Aluminium Producer in South East Asia and we embrace innovative technologies and push barriers a little further every time to constantly challenge ourselves.

Our Career
& Opportunities

Press Metal actively engages with emerging talents and envisage to provide career progression opportunities in tandem with the growth of the business globally. We will endeavour to extend opportunities to deserving individuals, you are after all, joining the family of South East Asia's largest aluminium producer!

Life at Press Metal

PRESS METAL is South East Asia’s largest aluminium producer and a globally integrated corporation with an international network covering China, the United Kingdom, North America and the Middle East.

We provide our employees with excellent opportunities for growth, training, advancement and employability as one of the country’s preferred employers.
