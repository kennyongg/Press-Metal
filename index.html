Enriching Lives For A Better Future

As we continue to grow, so do our efforts to attain sustainability.

"The generations to come and earth have something in common. Recycling is the right thing to do" -- Impactful Safer Tomorrow

Sustainability

Utilising renewable energy sources and a closed-loop recycling system to reduce environmental effects.

Conform to global carbon footprint standards
Hydroelectric adoption for energy generation
Using electricity efficiently for production
Endlessly recycling aluminium products
Backed by more than 25 years of experience
Global subsidiaries in China, Indonesia, and Australia
A smelting capacity of abvove 1 million tonnes per annum
Strong relationships with our customers.

Growth

As the largest aluminium producer in South East Asia with a global presence, we continue to grow.

Our Story

Press Metal started as a local aluminium extrusion company in 1986. Today, we're a world class producer of aluminium. We operate globally and serve many different industries, from food and beverage brands to technology giants.
